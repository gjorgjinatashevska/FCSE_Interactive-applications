How to connect to the Internet

While connecting to the internet might seem like a simple task, it can be complicated if you’re just getting started! We’ll cover three types of internet connections — Wi-Fi, ethernet, and dial-up. The process for connecting to a network varies depending on the type of connection and your device. This wikiHow will show you how to connect to the internet on Windows, Mac, Android, and iPhone.
